Token & Mechanics

CHOCO tokens are debased every Arbitrum block at a rate of roughly 7% per day. To compensate for this decrease, you can stake your tokens in the Smol Fridge or the Big Fridge vaults with a 24 hour lock and receive rewards. 'X' $TOKEN are minted every block and distributed to the vaults. The Smol Fridge is a single-sided staking vault which receives 10% of the rewards; the Big Fridge is an LP staking vault which receives 90% of the rewards. An additional vault, the Cryogenic Freezer, protects your CHOCO from debasing entirely with a 7 day lock.

Depositing or claiming will incur a 24 hour lock on withdrawals. Depositing or claiming again will reset the 24 hour timer, but not extend it. The debasement affects the entire token supply, including CHOCO tokens in the Smol and Big Fridge vaults and CHOCO in LP. Since CHOCO tokens in LP also get debased, the amount of CHOCO in these positions will decrease as the amount of ETH remains the same.

Diamond Hands  
Bonus rewards begin accumulating 25 hours after staking in the Smol Fridge (single-sided) or Big Fridge (LP). When this Diamond Hands mechanic begins, your APR will gradually increase up to a maximum of 1000% above baseline. Your bonus rewards will continue so long as you do not claim new CHOCO or sell any CHOCO. Whenever you claim, sell, or withdraw, your bonus APR will reset to zero and you will lose your Diamond Hands status.

Revenue Sharing  
Presale participants that contribute X ETH or more will get a choice of burning 0.3 ETH worth of their claimable CHOCO to receive a special revenue-sharing NFT alongside their remaining CHOCO. This NFT will enable them to collect a share of the protocol revenue from transaction taxes, which are set at 0.5% to 1.25% of all swaps (Hardcoded caps). The tax revenue will be divided equally among all wallets that hold a v2 NFT, with each being able to withdraw only their proportional part of the revenue pool. Other participants that did not contribute X ETH will be able to mint it for 0.5 ETH. There is no limit on the number of NFTs you can mint during the presale. NFTs of this type will never be minted again after the presale, but will be tradeable on the secondary market.
An additional pool for revenue sharing will potentially be opened for either an LP vault or the Cryogenic Freezer vault in the future - To Be Announced.
